Overview

A website dedicated to "Tiny Paws," an animal rescue organization. The website will feature details about the organization, profiles of adoptable animals, and an adoption form for potential adopters.

Site Name

Tiny Paws

Purpose

This website aims to connect rescued animals with loving homes by providing detailed profiles of adoptable pets and an easy-to-use adoption form. It also serves to spread awareness about the organization's mission and encourage donations.

Audience

The target audience includes animal lovers, potential adopters, and anyone interested in supporting animal welfare.

Scenarios

  • How can I adopt a dog or cat from "Tiny Paws"?
  • What animals are currently available for adoption?
  • How can I donate to support the rescue organization?

Core Features

  • Profiles of adoptable animals with photos and descriptions
  • An easy-to-complete adoption form stored temporarily using localStorage
  • Information about the organization's mission and recent adoptions
  • A call-to-action for donations to support the rescue efforts

Data Sources

  • Local Storage (for temporarily storing adoption form details)
  • Json Files (for dynamically displaying information on the page)

Dynamics

The homepage will dynamically display the latest animals available for adoption. The adoption form will also be interactive, with client-side validation to ensure a smooth user experience. When you click on a pet you can see their details and when you decide to adopt the pet will be added to a local storage list of pets. They will be displayed on the profile.

Animation Ideas

Hover effects on animal profiles, and animations for form submissions to enhance interactivity. The hamburger menu will display the navigation options by coming out of the side.
